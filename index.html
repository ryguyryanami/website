ALL I EVER WANTED EP

All I Ever Wanted EP is a preview of the upcoming album Everything is gonna be alright. The title track (All I ever wanted) is about the joy of dining with the people you love most, and the angst of sometimes not being able to. Deep Creek is about the Deep Creek Hot Springs, a hidden destination 2 hours outside of Los Angeles. Oh! is about a sudden realization. Everything is gonna be alright is a comforting reminder that although life can be stressful, things typically work out. We are not alone is a psychadelic-psychological horror track (with a comforting message!) about a connection with the divine, inspired by The Hateful Eight.

The Debut Full-Length

EVERYTHING IS GONNA BE ALRIGHT

An enlightening, invigorating, revitalizing, vivifying, enlivening, stimulating, and electrifying 13-track journey into the depths of what it means to live a good life. This album is intended to accompany prayer, laughter, reflection, tears, good food, and dance, all at the same time! Whistle and sing along to an array of eclectic melodies, and get lost in the cornucopia of harmonies begotten by the unique Ryanami voice. Focus on the present moment, grab a glass of wine, say a prayer of gratitude, round up your friends (maybe even someone to smooch!), and take upon yourself the Ryanami posture of being fully alive. Enjoy!
